SUGGESTIONS

1️⃣ Timeless Classics
📖 Pride and Prejudice – Jane Austen

A timeless romance and social commentary by Jane Austen.

📖 1984 – George Orwell

A dystopian masterpiece depicting a totalitarian regime where Big Brother watches everything.

📖 To Kill a Mockingbird – Harper Lee

A poignant novel exploring themes of racism, justice, and morality in the American South.

🚀 Science Fiction & Fantasy
🌌 Dune – Frank Herbert

Frank Herbert’s epic sci-fi masterpiece on politics and power.

🐉 The Hobbit – J.R.R. Tolkien

A whimsical fantasy tale following Bilbo Baggins on his unexpected journey to reclaim a lost treasure.

🌍 The Hitchhiker’s Guide to the Galaxy

A hilarious intergalactic adventure filled with absurd humor and philosophical musings.

🔍 Mystery & Thriller
🕵️‍♂️ The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo – Stieg Larsson

A gripping thriller featuring hacker Lisbeth Salander.

🔪 Gone Girl – Gillian Flynn

A psychological thriller with shocking twists, exploring marriage, media, and deception.

🧩 The Da Vinci Code – Dan Brown

A fast-paced thriller blending art, history, and religion in a global treasure hunt.

💡 Self-Improvement & Motivation
🎯 Atomic Habits – James Clear

Practical strategies for building good habits and breaking bad ones through small changes.

📈 The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People – Stephen R. Covey

A classic self-help book outlining principles for personal and professional success.

🧘 The Power of Now – Eckhart Tolle

A spiritual guide teaching mindfulness and living in the present moment.

🧠 Thought-Provoking Non-Fiction
📜 Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind – Yuval Noah Harari

Yuval Noah Harari’s deep dive into human evolution.

💰 The Psychology of Money – Morgan Housel

Insights into how human behavior shapes financial success and decision-making.

📊 Outliers – Malcolm Gladwell

Examines the factors that contribute to high levels of success, from talent to opportunity.
